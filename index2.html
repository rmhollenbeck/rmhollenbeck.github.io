History of Summer Break

The tradition of students having summer off from school dates back to a time when agriculture was a primary way of life for many families. In rural communities, children were often needed to help with planting and harvesting crops during the summer months. As a result, schools would close for the summer to accommodate this agricultural cycle.

July 4th

Independence Day is celebrated on July 4th and commemorates the adoption of the Declaration of Independence in 1776, declaring independence from Great Britain. It is a federal holiday marked by patriotic displays, fireworks, parades, and family gatherings.
