8th Grade Web Development Project

In this project, you will create a small website using HTML and CSS. Follow the directions below:

  1. Setup: Goto Lesson 20 in Code.org.
  2. HTML: In level 1, copy the contents of index.html from the link below into the index.html file.
  3. CSS: In level 1, copy the contents of style.css from the link below into the style.css file.
  4. Navigation: Customize the vertical navigation bar:
    • Add at least one link to a live page (it can be another page you create).
    • You may delete the directions from the navigation bar once your project is complete.
    • You may replace Font Awesome icons with ones of your choice. See Font Awesome Icons to pick icons.
    • Do not modify the vertical-navigation.css file.
  5. Content: Add meaningful content to your pages. You can include headings, paragraphs, images, and links.
  6. Style: You may modify style.css or add other .css files to change colors, fonts, spacing, and other visual aspects of your site.
  7. Submission: Make sure all links are working and all files are visible in Level 6 of Lesson 20.

Remember: Keep your vertical navigation bar intact. Focus on creating your own content and styling!
